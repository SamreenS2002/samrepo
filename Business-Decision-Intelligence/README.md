# Decision Science & Performance Analytics
**Project Goal:** Translating qualitative human factors into quantitative, actionable data.


**Employee Performance Matrix:** Evaluated staff using safety scores, drug testing results, and skills philosophy. 


**Analytical Results:** Calculated overall performance percentages, identifying individual strengths and areas for improvement. 


**Weighted Career Analysis:** Developed a decision-making model assigning weights to variables like Pay (Weight: 4), Enjoyment (Weight: 5), and Talent (Weight: 5). 


**Project Insight:** The scoring model mathematically identified 'Engineer' as the optimal career path with a top score of 55.
